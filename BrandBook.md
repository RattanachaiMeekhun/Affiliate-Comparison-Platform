# stacknodes - Brand Book

This document outlines the core branding principles and visual assets for **stacknodes**, a professional, AI-driven data aggregator platform.

---

## 1. Core Brand Identity

The brand identity focuses on clarity, professional data presentation, and a clean, modern aesthetic that builds trust with professional users.

- **Brand Name:** `stacknodes` (Written lowercase)
- **Tagline:** "AI-Powered Hardware Deals for Professionals"
- **Brand Personality:**
  - **Clean & Professional:** A bright, light-themed interface prioritizing readability.
  - **Trustworthy:** Data-driven, unbiased, and precise.
  - **Modern:** Utilizing subtle gradients, blur effects, and smooth animations.

---

## 2. Visual Identity

These guidelines are designed to create a modern, structured, and accessible user experience with a primary focus on Light Mode.

### 2.1 Color Palette

The core palette is designed for a pristine, professional user interface.

| Role                     | Name               | Hex / Variable        | Feeling                    |
| :----------------------- | :----------------- | :-------------------- | :------------------------- |
| **Main Background**      | **Pure White**     | `#ffffff`             | Clean, Spacious            |
| **Secondary Background** | **Light Gray**     | `#f8f9fa`             | Structure, Separation      |
| **Primary Brand**        | **Vibrant Blue**   | `#2563eb`             | Trust, Action, Tech        |
| **Primary Hover**        | **Deep Blue**      | `#1d4ed8`             | Interaction, Depth         |
| **Gradient Accent**      | **Blue to Purple** | `#2563eb` - `#7c3aed` | Modern, AI, Dynamic        |
| **Text Primary**         | **Dark Slate**     | `#111827`             | Maximum Readability        |
| **Text Secondary**       | **Muted Gray**     | `#4b5563`             | Hierarchy, Supportive text |

_(Note: The platform also features a dark hero section utilizing `#0f172a` and `#1e293b` for high-contrast introductions)._

### 2.2 Typography

- **Headings & Accents:**
  - Elegant serif fonts for a sophisticated, editorial touch (used selectively).
  - _Example:_ **Playfair Display**
- **Body & General UI:**
  - Clean, modern sans-serif fonts for optimal readability and technical data presentation.
  - _Example:_ **Inter**

### 2.3 Logo Concept

The logo should be minimal and recognizable.

- **Current usage:** A simple, rounded square icon (Primary Blue background) containing a lowercase white `s`, paired with the `stacknodes` typography in bold.
- **Style:** Clean, flat, and legible at small sizes.

---

## 3. UI/UX Principles

- **Glassmorphism:** Use of subtly blurred, semi-transparent backgrounds (e.g., sticky headers) to create depth without visual clutter.
- **Subtle Animations:** Elements like headers sliding in, or buttons shifting slightly on hover (`translateY(-1px)`), to provide a tactile feel.
- **Rounded Corners:** Consistent use of soft rounding (6px to 16px) for cards, buttons, and inputs to feel approachable.

---

## 4. Tone of Voice

Our communication style is concise, knowledgeable, and respectful of our audience's intelligence (data scientists, 3D artists, gamers, etc.).

- **Concise & Direct:** Focus on clarity. Explain hardware deals and methodology plainly.
- **Professional yet Approachable:** Builds trust through accurate data and clear presentation.
